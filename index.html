Chicken Soup Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 whole chicken (or 500g chicken breast), cut into pieces
  • 1.5 liters chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, carrots, and celery. Cook for about 5 minutes until softened.
  3. Add garlic and cook for 1 more minute.
  4. Add chicken pieces and chicken broth to the pot.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30â€“40 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot!

Enjoy your warm, homemade chicken soup!
